A narrow guide through my process:

Repeating the basics is always a great idea!

We started off learning the basics and state of the art practices.

Make a robot dance!

Our task was to visualize a song in a web project under the topic of «Dancing with Robots» I choose the song «Feel Good Inc.» as the music video inspired me every since I first saw it as a kid. The combination of animation (2D + 3D) and video footage I find fascinating. This I wanted to replicated on my website.

Getting Loud

The first step was getting the song to play. After watching a video by Ruth John on the WebAudio API I implemented the technology as a boombox on my song page.

New horizons

Learning new techniques from my teachers everyday, I used my newly gained skills to create this VHS-style effect to replicate the mood form the video.

Waves of Difficulty

Visualizing and therefore analysing it, proofed to be more difficult than expected. I experimented with P5 and the Web Audio API costing me alot of time. Nevertheless, it was an great milestone when I then managed to implement a waveform graphic via the WebAudio API.
